Africa's Legends Match 3

Africa's Legends Classic is an exhilarating fighting game set in the AL Universe, where players engage in thrilling turn-based match-3 battles. With a vast selection of legendary African characters, including Tolo, Oi Moran, Forger, Prophetess, Wudeng, Po, and Sundi, players embark on an epic journey to showcase their strategic prowess. In each turn, players must act swiftly as their timers rapidly collapse. By skillfully matching 3 or more identical gems, they can momentarily pause the timer and unleash powerful actions for their chosen character.

Sweave

The HOTTSEAT is a 'Who Wants to be a Millionaire' style trivia game engine with a back-end that is customizable. It is being deployed as an engagement and reward platform for use by brands, organizations, groups, banks, government, churches et cetera who would like to engage their users in a more interactive and fun way, while incentivizing them with various forms of rewards (cash, products, merchandise, virtual items, loyalty points, coupons). The HOTTSEAT has an intense and immersive gameplay daring players to play for a higher reward or to prove their knowledge about a topic area, hobby, product, service, profession or a company/institution. The game is available for play on most platforms including Web and Android with eventual support for iOS, Windows and SMS. Hottseat is currently licensed to MTN by Leti Arts as MTN Hottseat, and is a cross platform interactive game which gives brands the opportunity to engage with their clients. The quiz platform is hosted on the MTN Service Delivery Platform (SDP) and is integrated with objective questions on various subject areas as per GES’ curriculum, general knowledge and MTN product offerings for customers to respond to using a multiple-choice approach. MTN Hottseat has been designed similarly to the MTN branded TV show, “Who Wants to be Rich” which challenges players through 15 questions for points which can then be redeemed for rewards during promo periods
