Description

Welcome to Lakeside Design, an independent game studio driven by creativity, experimentation, and a love for immersive storytelling. Based in Bhopal, the City of Lakes, our name reflects both our roots and our philosophy—crafting games in a relaxed, stress-free environment where ideas can flow freely. At Lakeside Design, we believe in pushing the boundaries of game design through thoughtful mechanics and engaging narratives. Our goal is to bring unique and meaningful experiences to players by exploring ideas that have been simmering in our minds for years. We are a small but passionate team, currently working on two exciting projects: a word puzzle game that challenges language and logic and a detective narrative game that weaves an intricate mystery for players to unravel. As we grow, we remain committed to our core vision—creating games that are both innovative and personal, blending storytelling, design, and gameplay into something truly special. Join us on this journey as we explore the endless possibilities of game design, one idea at a time.

Words are your weapon, speed is your ally, and strategy is your key to survival. In this fast-paced word puzzle game, letters fall continuously from the top of the screen, creating an ever-growing challenge. Your goal? Form words before the letters pile up and reach the bottom. Clear lines, rack up combos, and use powerful boosts to bend the rules of language in your favor. Whether you’re a casual wordplay enthusiast or a competitive strategist, every second counts as you battle against time and gravity. Can you keep up with the falling letters and emerge as the ultimate word master?

C2

Embark on a captivating journey across the diverse landscapes of India in our detective narrative game, featuring two contrasting protagonists. Players can choose between a spiritually insightful detective, who employs ancient rituals and cultural wisdom to unravel enigmas, or an analytically sharp investigator, who utilizes modern technology and forensic psychology to crack cases. Each detective offers a distinct gameplay experience, with unique tools, methods, and perspectives, ensuring that every mystery is approached in a refreshingly different manner. Together, they delve into the nation’s deepest secrets, from unraveling ritualistic murders in secluded temples to exposing hidden truths behind urban legends, providing players with a rich tapestry of challenges and narratives to explore.
